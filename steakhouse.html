STEAK HOUSE


Ambiance
The restaurant has a distinct upmarket, yet casual feel to it. Wijnhuis is spacious - quite a large high room with some various nooks and alcoves, well designed for seating. Being a large group, we were seated in a private dining room though – with direct access to a private patio. Works for some; others prefer the hustle and bustle of a busy steakhouse.

Food
Dinner kicked off with plates of Bruschetta Prosciutto, Carpaccio and Calamari – all very good. Wijnhuis were thoughtful enough to compile and print our own bespoke steak menu - offering sirloin, fillet or sirloin-on-the-bone cuts. The 350gram fillet steak was priced at R308, which is notably expensive. However, just about everyone gave their steaks good scores.

Expectedly, Wijnhuis has an extensive selection of wines – and the wine list contains around 500 wines including collectors and vintage wines. Diners can also enjoy 20 wines available by the glass/carafe.

Service
We had two dedicated waitrons to take care of us, and they did a fantastic job. Always on hand, but never in your face.
